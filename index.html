⌘R Conf

To make you better at the things you love doing.

October 22-24, 2014
Shoreditch Village Hall, London
With from NSLondon

What is it?

⌘R Conf is a two-day, single track conference (plus a hack day) for serious iOS and OS X developers, looking to improve as developers, designers, creators and humans. There will be a focus on deep technical understanding, inclusive design and solving the harder problems we face as an industry.

Call for Papers

Interested in speaking at ⌘R Conf? We're looking for talks that tackle the harder problems facing our industry, whether they be serious technical challenges, moral and ethical quandaries, or design that goes beyond pure aesthetics.

We have a handful of full talk (30min) spots to fill for the main conference and some lighting talks (5-15 min). If you think you would like to be considered for either or both, please email us with your proposal, including title, abstract and a short biography by August 31, 2014.
